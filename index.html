Good to know

No insurance? That's OK.

All clinics listed here serve patients regardless of insurance status. Many use a sliding fee scale — you pay based on your income, often $0–$20 per visit.

You may qualify for Medicaid.

Virginia expanded Medicaid coverage. If you earn under ~$20,783/year, you likely qualify for free coverage. Apply at CoverVA.org.

Bring these to your visit.

Most clinics ask for a photo ID or green card, any current medications, and immunization records if available. You don't need insurance cards or proof of income at free clinics.

More resources exist.

This site lists a curated selection. For a full directory of 27+ Richmond-area free clinics, visit FreeClinics.com or the Virginia Association of Free Clinics.
